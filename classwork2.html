Cat Fancy Event 2014

The 115th annual Cat Fancy Event is being
held in the New York City on December 21st.
This is the fanciest cat event of the year,
starring over 300 different cats.This event
was made to show America how great cats
really are. There will be treat at work
in, and for the cat owners there will be a
buffet in the lounge. We thank you for
coming to our event site, and hope to see
you on December 21st. Thank you.
